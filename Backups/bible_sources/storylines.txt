Ally Quinn: True Crime Merge – 14-Day Mystery Storylines
Story 1: A Shadow in the Park
Day 1
Ally (narrating on her podcast): "Ten years ago, high school senior Mia Donovan was found murdered in Brookford Park. I'll help however I can."Merge Clue: Old Case File – (Players merge torn documents to reassemble the original police case file, unlocking background details of the 2015 investigation.)

Day 10
