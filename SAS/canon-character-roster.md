## Explicitly ruled OUT of canon (2026-07-11)

- **Middle names** for the cast (e.g. "Thomas Edward", "Arthur Elias", "Dante Aurelius", "Dana Elise") — sourced from no document; rejected. Middle names get minted deliberately when a story beat needs one.
- Danielle H. (player persona), Marlow & Lena (old FTUE prototypes), agent-squad names (Shaun, Archie, Nico…) — production artifacts, not in-world people.
- Unnamed functional walk-ons (mayor, bus driver, retired Harbourline mechanic, diner staff) — not roster characters unless a script names them.
- "Sabine Rourke has any relation to Mags O'Rourke" — she does not; the surname echo is coincidence, not lineage.

## Rules for extending this roster

1. New character in a script → added here at approval, marked *proposed* until a portrait is approved.
2. First approved portrait = canon appearance, recorded here and in `portrait-prompts-canon-cast.md`.
3. External summaries (ChatGPT etc.) are audited against this doc — anything not traceable here or to a script is treated as invention until deliberately adopted.
