7. **Game Systems as Narrative** — merge → leads → dialogue → flags → resolution loop; requirement/economy bands; item families & their character ties; generators; evidence board; energy & monetisation (Platinum Ingots, ladder, 4 SKUs) — summary + pointers to SAS masters
8. **Production Canon Rules** — canon-first; first-approved-image; blue-is-lighting; temperature grammar; Ferryman DO-NOT-GENERATE; naming conventions; doc hierarchy (bible ↔ roster ↔ prompts ↔ scripts)
9. **Appendices** — glossary; narrative-flag registry; asset naming; source-document archaeology (what superseded what, so nobody re-mines stale docs unknowingly); **Parked Ideas Vault** (see preservation rule); Episode Seed Vault

**★ PRESERVATION RULE (Stephen, 2026-07-11):** *"DROP" never means delete.* Any idea with plausible long-term merit — even if out of current scope — is preserved in the **Parked Ideas Vault** appendix with a one-line description and its source. Examples already qualifying: crime-type taxonomy beyond Ep1's kind, the 4-part episode skeleton variants, marketing/UA plays not yet used, deferred features (branching depth, minigames, new item families reviewers proposed), old-bible lore fragments that didn't fit a renamed character. Only true duplicates and superseded *numbers* (old balancing data) are dropped without preservation.

Target: 15–20k words (may run higher with Full AQ Download mined — quality over ceiling). Master: `SAS/ally-quinn-bible.md`. Exports: styled .docx to `Bible/` + PDF. Old docx archived, never deleted.
